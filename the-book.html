When Vice President JD Vance spots an alarming headline warning about a troll causing trouble at 1600 Pennsylvania Avenue, he knows he has to investigate. As a proud Marine veteran, JD is ready to protect the White House and get to the bottom of this mystery.

Join JD on his investigation as he searches for clues on the White House grounds, reads intelligence reports, meets a very busy Marine guard, and finally discovers the truth behind the glowing Oval Office door. But the troll he finds isn't what anyone expected!

With charming illustrations and a clever twist ending, Who's Trolling the White House? is a delightful picture book mystery that teaches young readers about the importance of investigating for themselves, thinking critically about what they hear, and discovering that the truth is often more interesting than the headline.
