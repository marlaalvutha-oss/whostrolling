About the Authors

J

John Howland

John Howland is a proud American patriot who believes in the power of humor, storytelling, and teaching the next generation to love our great country and think for themselves.

Who's Trolling the White House? is his first children's book, combining a deep love of America, respect for our military heroes, conservative values, and the classic picture book mystery format. He created this book to help families share the joy of a great story while teaching kids about American courage, military service, and the importance of always investigating for yourself before jumping to conclusions.
